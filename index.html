Monday
Push — Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
AMRAP▼
15–20 min
No equipment
3–5 rounds

Move through each exercise back-to-back without stopping. Rest only after a full round. Chest to floor on every push-up, arms to full extension at the top.

One Round
Push-Ups
Hands slightly wider than shoulders. Chest to floor, lock out at top. Core tight — no sagging hips.
Can't do 5? → Incline push-ups on a chair, or knee push-ups
10
reps
Pike Push-Ups
Hips high, head toward floor. Triangle between hands and head at bottom. Targets shoulders.
Too hard? → Negative pike: lower slowly, skip the press up
6
reps
Dips
Use two chairs. Elbows to 90°, then press to locked position. Slow descent — don't swing.
Too hard? → Bench dips with feet flat on floor
8
reps
High-to-Low Plank
Push-up position → lower one elbow, then the other → press back up. Core tight throughout.
6
reps
Form CueInhale on the way down, exhale on the press. Never fully lock elbows — slight bend protects the joints.

Wednesday
Pull + Core — Back · Biceps · Abs
AMRAP▼
15–20 min
Pull-up bar
3–5 rounds

Pull movements and core work. Hold the bar at the base of your fingers, not the palm — prevents wrist strain and improves grip. Quality over quantity.

One Round
Pull-Ups
Full hang to chin over bar. Engage scapula on the way up. Slow controlled descent — don't drop.
Can't do 1? → Australian pull-ups (bar at hip height, body angled)
5
reps
Chin-Ups
Underhand grip, shoulder-width. Focus on the bicep squeeze at the top.
Too hard? → Jump to top, lower slowly over 3–5 sec
5
reps
Hanging Knee Raises
From dead hang, pull knees to chest using core. Lower slowly — no swinging.
No bar? → Lying leg raises on the floor (lower back flat)
8
reps
Plank Hold
Elbows under shoulders, body rigid. Squeeze everything — no raised hips, no sag.
30s
hold
Progression RuleWhen you can do 10 pull-ups with perfect form, move up — slow the descent to 3 sec, or try L-sit pull-ups.
